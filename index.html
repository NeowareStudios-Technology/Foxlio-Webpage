  The Web 3.0

The internet is constantly evolving and the Blockchain is the next iteration of the web.

Through decentralization, anonymity and security the road to the future of the web is being paved.

  More Than Security

Blockchains are decentralized using a digital ledger to enable online transactions securely.

This digital ledger records the transactions across many computers to prevent the alteration of each block.

  Future Currencies

Through the Blockchain the creation of a new form of currency came about, Cryptocurrency.

The future is secure, decentralized and often time anonymous.

  Decentralized

Computers all over the world use blockchain to work together and record Bitcoin transactions.

This means there is no one central authority over the network.

  Access to Everyone

With billions of people around the world, around 2.2 billion of those have access to the internet but not the normal exchanges.

In fact 1 in every 3 Kenyans own a Bitcoin wallet.
